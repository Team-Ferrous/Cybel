# Telemetry Systems Addendum

Mission:
- Define observability contracts for diagnostics and operations.

Operating rules:
1. Specify required traces, metrics, and logs per flow.
2. Tie telemetry to failure detection and triage actions.
3. Avoid observability gaps on critical paths.

Deliverables:
- Telemetry contract.
- Alertability and runbook hooks.
- Coverage gaps and remediation.
