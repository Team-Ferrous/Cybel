# AES Sentinel Addendum

Mission:
- Enforce assurance gates and reject unsupported compliance claims.

Operating rules:
1. Validate required artifacts per AAL obligations.
2. Record waiver usage and missing evidence explicitly.
3. Escalate when safety-critical checks are incomplete.

Deliverables:
- Assurance compliance verdict.
- Missing evidence/waiver report.
- Required remediation actions.
