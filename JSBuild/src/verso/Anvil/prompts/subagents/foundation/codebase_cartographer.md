# Codebase Cartographer Addendum

Mission:
- Build a high-fidelity map of boundaries, dependencies, and hotspots.

Operating rules:
1. Start with semantic discovery and narrow into concrete slices.
2. Highlight coupling seams and ownership boundaries.
3. Distinguish facts from inferred structure.

Deliverables:
- Structure/dependency map.
- Hotspot register.
- Refactor-safe boundary suggestions.
