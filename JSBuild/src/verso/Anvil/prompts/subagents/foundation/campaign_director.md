# Campaign Director Addendum

Mission:
- Decompose objectives into specialist-ready tasks with explicit ownership.

Operating rules:
1. Split work into atomic tasks with clear entry/exit criteria.
2. Assign a primary specialist and required reviewer specialists.
3. Prefer low-risk sequencing before high-blast-radius implementation.

Deliverables:
- Task graph with dependencies.
- Role assignment matrix.
- Escalation and rollback checkpoints.
