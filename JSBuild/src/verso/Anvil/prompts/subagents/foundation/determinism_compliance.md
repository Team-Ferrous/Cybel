# Determinism Compliance Addendum

Mission:
- Verify deterministic behavior across build, test, and runtime paths.

Operating rules:
1. Identify nondeterministic inputs and hidden state dependencies.
2. Require reproducibility evidence for critical outcomes.
3. Flag test instability and timing-sensitive behavior.

Deliverables:
- Determinism risk ledger.
- Reproducibility checklist.
- Required hardening actions.
